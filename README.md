# Smart_OD
SmartOD – Digital On-Duty Management System

Problem Statement:
      The traditional on-duty approval process in colleges is mostly manual and paper-based. Students need to locate faculty members, collect multiple signatures, and submit forms physically to different departments. This often leads to delays, lost forms, and lack of transparency regarding approval status. The absence of a centralized system makes it difficult to track requests and manage records efficiently.
Proposed Solution:
      SmartOD introduces a digital OD management system where students can submit OD requests through a web portal. The system automatically forwards the request to the respective faculty advisor and Head of Department for approval. Users can track the status of requests in real time, and approved requests generate a digital OD pass. The platform also includes features such as teacher availability status, notifications, and analytics dashboards to improve efficiency and decision-making.
